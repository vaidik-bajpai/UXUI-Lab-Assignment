Learning Resource Center (LRC) @ JIET

About LRC

The Learning Resource Center of JIET is a major activity center providing facilities to the students and staff for almost 15 hours a day...

Library Facilities

  • The library is fully classified and cataloged.
  • Users can search for information at their own using the Alice for Windows Library management software.
  • OPAC (Online Public Access Catalogue) is available for checking the availability status of books and materials.
  • Linked with DELNET (Developing Library Network) for wider access to reading materials.
  • Subscription to journals of national and international repute.
  • Photocopying facilities within the library.

Library Resources

The Center has the latest books and journals of International and National standard covering all branches of science and Engineering offered by the institute...
